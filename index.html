About Me

I’m Adarsh SR, a cybersecurity enthusiast and competitive CTF player with a strong focus on ethical hacking, reverse engineering, and system security. I’m currently pursuing a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Applications while actively pushing the boundaries of offensive security through hands-on practice and research.

I’m the founder of TRIADA, one of India’s top-ranked CTF teams, and a core team member at VULNCON, a national-level cybersecurity conference dedicated to exploring vulnerabilities, ethical hacking, and digital defense strategies. It brings together professionals, students, and enthusiasts to share knowledge, showcase skills, and foster innovation in the infosec community.
